## 🌟 Overview

Welcome to **Emad\'s Modpack**, your gateway to an unparalleled Minecraft 1.21.1 experience! This carefully crafted modpack combines **superior performance**, **stunning visual enhancements**, and **essential quality-of-life additions** to deliver a rich and enjoyable world without compromise.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### ⚡ Unmatched Performance
Bid farewell to lag! Optimized for unprecedented smoothness even in large worlds.
- **Sodium & Lithium:** Radical graphical and physical performance improvements.
- **C2ME:** Improved world generation and chunk loading.

### ✨ Captivating Visuals
Immerse yourself in a vibrant world with improved animations and realistic lighting.
- **Fresh Animations:** Brings life to entities and creatures.
- **Iris Shaders:** Support for breathtaking shaders.
- **Visual Armor Trims:** Visually display armor trims.

### 🛠️ Quality of Life
Streamline daily tasks and provide valuable information without spoiling the vanilla experience.
- **Jade:** Detailed info about blocks and entities.
- **Xaero\'s Maps:** Detailed mini and world maps.
- **AppleSkin:** Accurate hunger and saturation info.
